What is Phoenix?

Phoenix is ​​a platform for assisting in the communication of teenagers that suffers from depression. The project's main objective is to enable greater interaction between the patient and the psychologist, assisting the psychologist in the frequent monitoring of the patient's condition and promoting a comfortable environment for the teenagers with depression to talk about their routine. The Phoenix Project was developed by Computer Technician students of the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology Sul-Rio-Grandense, Sapucaia do Sul campus.
